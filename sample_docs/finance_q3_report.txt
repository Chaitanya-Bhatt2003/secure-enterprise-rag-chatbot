ACME CORPORATION — Q3 FY2026 FINANCIAL REPORT (INTERNAL)
Prepared by: Finance Department | Classification: Confidential

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Q3 revenue reached $12.4M, up 18% year-over-year, driven primarily by the
enterprise subscription tier. Gross margin improved to 71% from 68% in Q2.
Operating expenses grew 9%, mainly from cloud infrastructure scaling and
two executive hires. Net income for the quarter was $1.9M.

REVENUE BREAKDOWN
- Enterprise subscriptions: $7.1M (57% of revenue)
- SMB subscriptions: $3.2M (26%)
- Professional services: $1.4M (11%)
- Marketplace and other: $700,000 (6%)

The top three enterprise accounts (Northwind Logistics, Contoso Health,
Fabrikam Manufacturing) together represent $2.8M of quarterly revenue.
Contract renewal for Northwind ($1.1M annually) is due in Q1 FY2027 and
is rated at-risk pending the SLA remediation plan.

EXPENSES
- Payroll and benefits: $5.6M
- Cloud infrastructure: $1.8M (AWS reserved instance renegotiation saved
  $210,000 this quarter)
- Sales and marketing: $1.3M
- Facilities and other: $600,000

BUDGET FORECAST — Q4 FY2026
Q4 revenue is forecast at $13.5M ± 5%. The engineering department budget
increases by $400,000 to fund the vector-search infrastructure project.
A hiring freeze applies to all non-engineering, non-quota-carrying roles
until January 2027.

RISKS
1. Foreign exchange exposure on EUR-denominated contracts (~$1.2M).
2. Concentration risk: top 5 customers are 31% of revenue.
3. Pending SOC 2 Type II audit; remediation budget of $150,000 reserved.
